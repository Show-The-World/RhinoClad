Our Patio Doors

For three decades, Rhino Windows and Doors has been the trusted name in patio doors, offering both competitive pricing and unparalleled customer service. Our dedication to quality and performance sets us apart in the industry. Each patio door undergoes rigorous testing in our in-house lab, ensuring that only the finest products reach our customers. With Rhino, you're not just getting a patio door; you're investing in lasting craftsmanship and reliability.

Patio Door Options

Crafted with precision from the foundation upwards, our doors offer:


  • A robust 5-7/8″ mechanical frame for enduring strength.

  • A securely welded sash fortified with steel.

  • A multi-chambered frame, enhancing stability and optimizing thermal performance.

  • The choice of optional aluminum sills in either sleek black or refined anodized finishes.

  • The flexibility of dual glazing or the enhanced insulation of triple glazing options.
