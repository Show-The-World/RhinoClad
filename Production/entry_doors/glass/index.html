Glass

At Rhino, our commitment to excellence is evident in every piece of glass we craft. Our decorative glass panels are a testament to our dedication, echoing the timeless beauty of stained glass. Each panel is meticulously handcrafted, ensuring that every design resonates with elegance and precision. From the selection of premium art glass to the intricate hand-cut bevels, we ensure that our designs are both visually stunning and built to last.

Glass Styles

Every piece of decorative pattern glass we create is unique, handcrafted with attention to every detail. This process might introduce slight imperfections or variations in texture, but these are inherent characteristics of handcrafted glass. Whether it's bubbles, seeds, or subtle surface flaws, each variation adds to the character of the design. Some patterns, like Swirl and Drawn, might have more of these unique inclusions, but rest assured, they're not defects but signatures of authenticity.
